The Photon Sciences Users' Executive Committee at Brookhaven National Laboratory presents:

National Synchrotron Light Source II

Long Island's State of the Art X-Ray microscope

  • Design and Artwork by: Linnea Russell
  • Concept and Text by: Scott Calvin and Bruce Ravel

This web site contains the source materials and building instructions for a pop-up book about the NSLS-II synchtotron.

Have you lost or damaged a piece from your book? Print out and fold a replacement!

Do you want to make your own copy? Download the image files, print them, and follow the construction instructions.

Do you want to improve on the book or modify it for your own needs? Download the Photoshop save files.
